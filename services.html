The Full List of
services that Plus St Hosts.

The core of Plus St are the dynamic and static content we host. From messaging, utilities, and social networks, we have a ton of internet services for you to freely use!

Private Bin!

Private bin is a private, minimalist, zero knowledge pastebin with password lock, one time viewing, and more!

SearXNG!

SearXNG is a meta search engine that's private and open source!

Libreddit!

Libreddit is a private frontend for people who prefer Reddit's modern layout, written in rust!

Matrix!

Matrix is a federated protocol often used for messaging, but it can be used for way more!

Gitea!

Gitea is a painless self hostable Git service that is easy to use and migrate to Start coding on gitea today!

XMPP!

XMPP is a federated instant messaging, it's flexible, secure, and private!

Akkoma!

Akkoma is a fork of Pleroma, a federated network, with extra features and improvments. No ads and no corperate greed!
