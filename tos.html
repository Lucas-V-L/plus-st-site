Terms of Service.

As Plus St hosts services for public usage, a Terms of Use needed to properly outline what to do and not to do on Plus St.

By using Plus St and any service that we host you are agreeing to the Terms of Service outlined on this webpage.

#1 Prohibited Actions.

This section outlines what you cannot do with Plus St's services.

  • #1.1 Spreading and promoting the use of hate speech.

  • #1.2 Spreading and promoting content related to underage children, including artistic depictions of such content.

  • #1.3 Stalking, abusing, spamming, doxing, impersonating, and harassing another user.

  • #1.4 Excessive advertising and accounts that focus on a company.

  • #1.5 Posting adult content without censoring it as NSFW, having an NSFW avatar, and an NSFW banner.

#2 Fediblock Policy.

This section outlines how we handle the moderation of external users and instances breaking the TOS.

  • #2.1 Instances that break TOS sections #1.1, #1.2, and #1.3 will be blocked.

  • #2.2 Instances that break TOS sections #1.4 and #1.5 will be unlisted.

  • #2.3 Instances that break TOS section #1.5 will have their media forced as NSFW, Avatars removed, and Banners removed.

#3 If you break TOS.

This section outlines how we handle the moderation of users breaking the TOS.

  • #3.1 If you break TOS sections #1.1, #1.2, #1.3, and #1.4 your account will be deactivated.

  • #3.2 If you break TOS section #1.5, your account will receive a warning, and repeated violations will result in an account deactivation.
